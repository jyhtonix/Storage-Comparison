Quick Summary

💿 Hard Disk Drive

  • Magnetic platters + R/W heads
  • Rotating mechanical storage
  • Highest capacity per dollar
  • Suitable for cold/bulk storage

📀 Optical Disk

  • Laser reads pits & lands
  • CD / DVD / Blu-ray variants
  • Archival, media distribution
  • Read-only or write-once

⚡ SATA SSD

  • NAND Flash + SATA interface
  • No moving parts, silent
  • Drop-in HDD replacement
  • Limited by SATA 6Gbps cap

🚀 NVMe SSD

  • NAND Flash + PCIe interface
  • NVMe protocol, low latency
  • Best sequential & random I/O
  • Enterprise & gaming flagship
